Melatonin

Waa maxay Melatonin?

Melatonin waa hormoon hurdada caawiya oo loo isticmaalo hurdo la’aanta.

Sida loo qaato

Qaado fiidkii, dhowr saac ka hor intaadan seexan ama 1-2 saac ka hor intaa seexan.

Digniino

  • Waxay keeni kartaa hurdo badan, madax-wareer ama lallabbo khafiif ah.

Dhibaatooyinka (Bivirkninger)

  • Hurdo badan ama daal subaxeed.
  • Madax-xanuun ama wareer.
  • Calool-wareem ama lallabbo khafiif ah.

Is-dhexgalka daawooyinka (Interaktioner)

  • Daawooyinka hurdo-dejiya kale waxay kordhin karaan hurdo badan – la tasho dhakhtarka ka hor intaadan isku darin.
  • Aalkoladu waxay dhaawacdaa hurdo tayadeeda; waa in laga fogaado marka la qaadanayo Melatonin.
  • Daawooyinka wadnaha qaarkood ama cadaadiska dhiigga waxay la falgali karaan Melatonin – dhakhtarkaaga u sheeg daawo kasta oo aad qaadato.

Ramadaanka iyo daawada

La hadal dhakhtarkaaga ka hor Ramadaanka si loo hubiyo waqtiga ugu habboon ee qaadashada Melatonin.

Badanaa waxaa la qaataa fiidkii (afurka kadib). Ha beddelin waqtiga adiga oo aan la tashan dhakhtar.
